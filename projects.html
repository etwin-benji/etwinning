Our Projects

This page documents the main projects developed under the NOVA initiative. Each project focuses on research, experimentation, and long-term educational value.

Active Projects

AI Research Hub

The AI Research Hub is a long-term project dedicated to exploring artificial intelligence, machine learning models, and their ethical implications.

Topics include neural networks, natural language processing, computer vision, and responsible AI deployment.

Research AI

Global Data Analysis

This project focuses on collecting, cleaning, and analyzing public datasets from global institutions and open-data platforms.

The goal is to transform complex datasets into understandable insights using visualization techniques.

Data Visualization

Open-Source Toolkit

A continuously expanding collection of reusable components, utilities, and templates designed for modern web development.

All tools are documented and shared openly to support learning and collaboration.
